The ICT field

The Information and communication (ICT) sector is one of the most rapidly evolving sectors in the EU. ICT professionals, who mainly work in the IT sector, accounted for 2 percent of all employment in the EU for 2022. Professionals in this field develop and improve information technologies like software, hardware and digital databases. Also they provide advice to other professionals and people who are just getting into ICT. To sum things up. ICT is constantly evolving and there is never-ending waves of new people eager to join the vast field of ICT.
