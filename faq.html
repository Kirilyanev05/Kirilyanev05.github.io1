FAQ

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

1. Go to a HZ printer

2. Log in to your account

3. Select the document you want to print

4. Pay with your HZ card

5. Print!

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

1. Go to a HZ printer

2. Log in

3. Place your document on the scanner

4. The scanned document should be sent to your email

How can you book a project space?

1. Go to the home page of the Self Service Portal

2. Click on Reservations

3. Choose the appropriate category and search for the "resource" you want to reserve. If necessary, use the Type filter to turn certain items on/off in the overview.

How can I buy something on the HZ web shop?

Simply place your order via the Self Service Portal on MyHZ.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Pay attention to where you park your car: spots for electric cars, contractors, HZ cars, or people with disabilities and/or chronic conditions are marked with signs and only intended for that target group.

It is not allowed to park (partially) on spaces with yellow lines.
