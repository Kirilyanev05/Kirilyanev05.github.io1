A little bit about myself:

I was born in Bulgaria, in a small town named Vidin. Growing up in Vidin, I was surrounded by the rich history and culture that the town has to offer. I have deep appreciation for history. This unique environment has shaped my values and perspectives, instilling in me a deep appreciation for my roots. The large number of historical landmarks in Vidin, are the reason I am so fond of history and discovering new things.

Despite never attending a school that specializes in programming, my curiosity about computers and how they work has always driven me to explore the world of technology. The person that inspired me to join the ICT program was my father, who taught me everything he knew about computers from an early age. I still remember the first time he taught me how to build a computer.

My characteristics:

  • Hardworking and determined
  • Passionate about technology
  • Creative problem-solver
  • Good communicator and team player
  • Eager to learn new things
