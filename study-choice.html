My study choice

Choosing a field of study was probably one of the hardest desicions in my life. I went from studying Law to Psycology, Programming and then back to Law. I couldn't make my mind what I should study and I was ready to give up. But thanks to my family and friends I managed to put my mind together and probably made the best desicion of my life. I chose ICT. My family helped me realise that is really for me. Not only, because I like computers and games but also because this is what I am good at. Also I am really optimistic about my future, because I know that with the knowledge and skills that I will gain in ICT, I will be able to make a career in this field of work.
