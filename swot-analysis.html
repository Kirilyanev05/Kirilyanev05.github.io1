My SWOT analysis

A SWOT analysis is a powerful tool for self-reflection and I am glad that this course gave me the opportunity to do one myself. I would first like to start with my strengths. Even though I am still very young and have much to learn, I can confidently say that I have quite a few strengths. One of them is my adaptability. I can safely say that since I arrived in The Netherlands I have adapted quite well in this new environment for me. Also this will help me in the field of ICT because this is a crucial skill in this field. Lets move on to my weaknesses. I am a human after all and no one can be perfect. Probably my greatest wekness is my tendency to procrastinate. This has always been an issue of mine and even though I am working on it and have improved, I still suffer from it. Looking forward in the future there are several opportunities for growth and advancments. This is also one of the reason why I chose ICT, because this field is rapidly expanding everyday, which unlocks a lot of opportunities. If you know what you are doing of cource. Lastly, this article could not be completed without mentioning the threats I need to navigate. One such threat is the fast-paced nature of ICT. One could easily lose track of things and fall behind. People in this field of work should always stay updated on the latest developments.
